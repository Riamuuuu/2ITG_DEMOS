About us

Our team consist of 7 people who likes to share stories and spread awareness. We are students from a university located in Manila, Philippines and we're working hard to build a legacy in this world. We always aimed to do things uniquely and we always aim higher.

Mission & Vision

Mission

This advocacy aims to spread awareness and educate people about misinformation and disinformation by creating this website and sharing all our knowledge. We would like to grab this opportunity to help people check what is true and what is fake in the internet world.

Vision

We envision a community wherein people are well-aware and well-informed about what is happening around them.
